About Me

I'm Adittya Das, a Mechanical Engineering undergraduate at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. I completed my SSC from Chattogram Government High School and HSC from Ispahani Public School and College, both with a GPA of 5.00.

My interests lie deeply in thermal systems and materials science. I'm currently building my skills in Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, and CoolProp — still a rookie in most of these, but learning fast and enjoying every step of it.

On the design side, I'm confident with AutoCAD and SolidWorks.

Bot

Four days. One bot. Absolute chaos. Built for the Soccer-Bot competition in our second term, this project was an exercise in problem-solving under pressure. We ran into constant issues — bad solder joints, code that refused to compile on Arduino, a desperate midnight switch to ESP, and frantic help sessions with seniors. But the morning of the competition, the bot was alive and ready. We finished 4th place — and it felt like winning. Same team, even more battle-hardened.
